INFORMED CONSENT — UI Preference Study

You are invited to participate in a brief research study on user interface design preferences.

WHAT YOU WILL DO:
You will view 6 pairs of user interface designs and select the one you prefer in each pair. You will also rate your confidence in each choice. The study takes approximately 3-5 minutes.

VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION:
Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may stop at any time by closing your browser. No penalty will result from withdrawal.

ANONYMITY:
No personally identifiable information is collected. Responses are recorded with a randomly generated anonymous ID. Data will be reported only in aggregate.

DATA USE:
Your anonymous responses will be used for class research purposes and may be included in academic presentations or publications. Only group-level results will be reported.

RISKS:
This study involves minimal risk. You will only be viewing interface designs and clicking your preferences.

CONTACT:
If you have questions about this study, please contact: hashexplaindata (hashexplaindata@users.noreply.github.com)

By proceeding, you confirm that you are 18 years of age or older and consent to participate.
